Is the PDC certification globally recognized?

Yes, our Permaculture Design Certificate follows the 72-hour international standard established by Bill Mollison. Our graduates are recognized by design institutes worldwide.

Do I need previous experience?

No! We have courses ranging from total beginners to professional-grade design workshops. We'll guide you from your first seed to your first masterplan.
