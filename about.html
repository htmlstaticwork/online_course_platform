Our Roots

We are a global community of educators, designers, and earth-stewards dedicated to the principles of regenerative living.

Our Mission

To empower individuals worldwide with the design tools, ecological knowledge, and community support needed to transition towards a regenerative solar-based culture.

Our Vision

A world where human systems act as a catalyst for biodiversity, soil health, and hydrological resilience, creating abundance for all species.

The Beginning

A Heritage of
Stewardship.

Gaia Academy didn't start in a boardroom. It began in the deep mulch of a 30-year-old food forest, where our founders realized that ecological wisdom must be shared as a heritage, not just a set of techniques.
